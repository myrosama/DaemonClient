ENCRYPTION

Your device encrypts.
Your cloud stores.

Files are split into chunks, each encrypted with AES-256-GCM on your device before upload. The server receives ciphertext it cannot interpret. Your keys never leave your machine.

Key derivation is local. Every chunk gets a unique nonce. Authentication tags verify integrity on every download.

Privacy

Your files are
sealed on your device.
We literally cannot
read your files.

Zero-knowledge is not a marketing claim here. Encryption keys are derived locally from your credentials. Our servers store only ciphertext. Even a court order would yield nothing interpretable.

ZERO_KNOWLEDGE

No server-side keys

Key derivation happens entirely on your device. Our servers handle only encrypted bytes — never your keys, never your plaintext, never your metadata in cleartext.

AES-256-GCM

Encrypted before upload

Every chunk is independently encrypted with a unique nonce. Authentication tags detect any tampering. NIST-standard, widely audited, and resistant to near-term quantum attacks.

OPEN_SOURCE

Verify every claim

The entire client — encryption logic, chunking, key derivation — is public code. Don't trust our word. Read it, fork it, audit it. The server is yours anyway.
